In An Emergency, Who Knows You Need Help?

If power or cell service is down during an emergency, how will first responders know who needs help?

Every street has a Block Captain who checks on neighbors and reports emergencies to the city using HAM radio. Expect a visit from your Block Captain in the event of an emergency such as a natural disaster. Be suspicious of anyone you don't know who claims to be from the government or first responders.

To find out who your Block Captain is, or to volunteer as a Block Captain, call Kelvin Grove resident Kelly Veater at 801-358-5089.
